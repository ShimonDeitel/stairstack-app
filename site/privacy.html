Privacy Policy

Stairstack is an offline-first app. All of your entries are stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support folder and are never transmitted anywhere. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The app makes no network requests except the standard App Store transaction check used to verify Pro subscription status through Apple's StoreKit, which is handled entirely by Apple.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. We have no servers and no way to access your data.
